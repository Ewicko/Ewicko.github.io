I presented "Generative Design for Compliant Mechanisms Using Agentic AI" at the 2025 SIAM International Conference in Montreal, Quebec. This work explores the integration of reinforcement learning with generative design to accelerate the design of compliant mechanisms, achieving significant computational speedups (130-200 agent steps PER SECOND while training, with ultrafast linear FEA running in 30-40 miliseconds).
